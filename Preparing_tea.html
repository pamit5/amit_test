Ingredients Required

To prepare two cups of tea
  • 2 tea spoon tea leaves
  • 4 tea spoon sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup water

How to Prepare Tea?
  1. Heat the water in a vessel.
  2. Add the sugar and tea leaves and let it boil.
  3. Pour in the milk and bring it to a boil.
  4. Filter the tea into two cups.

Note: For additional flavor, you can add a small piece of ginger or cardamom.
